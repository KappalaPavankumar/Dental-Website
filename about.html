More About Dentist

Dr Piyusha S Patil DDS, D.M.S.C

Dr.Piyusha S Patil, DDS, is a passionate dentist excited to show patients how a trip to the office for dental work can be a wonderful experience. His practice philosophy is simple: to provide quality dentistry in a caring, respectful, efficient, and professional manner. SHe feels fortunate to have a team of professionals whose philosophies of dentistry align with his. They all work together for the health and comfort of their patients at Dental Care & Orthodontics in Garland, Texas.

Dr. Piyusha S Patil and his exceptional team work hard to create an environment where patients feel comfortable, respected, and cared for. When she's not in the office, Dr. Piyusha S Patil is in the garage working on projects — strengthening her hands and perfecting his technique. She is a member of the Texas Inventors Association. Dr. Piyusha S Patil and her husband Steve are also the proud parents of two young children.
